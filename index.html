In Loving Memory of Kent Germain

13th April 1956 - 10th September 2024

A collection of memories to celebrate a life well-lived.

"A life beautifully lived deserves to be beautifully remembered."

About Kent

Kent was a kind, generous soul who touched the lives of everyone he met. He was a loving father, devoted family man who will be deeply missed by all who knew him. Kent was a talented cricket player, a dedicated herbsman, and a cherished friend. His legacy will live on in the hearts of those who loved him.

Memorial service at Greenford Park Cemetery Chapel, Windmill Lane, Greenford, Middlesex, UB6 9DU on 10th October 2024
